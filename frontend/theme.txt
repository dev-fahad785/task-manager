Typography
Choose geometric sans-serif fonts with a sci-fi feel for headings, paired with a clean readable body font. Research notes that “futuristic fonts offer a blend of high-tech and experimental elements. Most are sans serif with geometric shapes, sharp angles, or sci-fi-inspired details”
figma.com
. For instance, a display font like Orbitron or Audiowide (with angular forms) can headline section titles, while a simpler sans like Roboto or Montserrat handles paragraphs. Keep body text light gray (not pure white) for comfort

. Also ensure text contrasts meet accessibility – e.g. use ~87% white on black for headings and ~60% for body

. In short, a bold, “space-age” font for key titles and a neutral sans for content will reinforce the futuristic theme.
